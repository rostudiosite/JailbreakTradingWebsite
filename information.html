Information
At JB Values, we make every effort to keep things as transparent as possible. We never want to hide or abuse anything, therefore we make sure all values are accurate and up to date. We have a team that monitors and adjusts the numbers on a frequent basis. Here we will discuss all of our approaches for gathering and updating information.
How do we find values?
Having values that are decided by the community will result in them being inaccurate. This is due to players valuing items they own more than others, and valuing them by what they think rather than what is actually true. We have figured out that the best way to get accurate values is trying to get test-trades done. If you don't know what it means then I will explain it.
Test trades
Test trades are a method of obtaining the correct values. This entails us communicating with users about the possibility of a transaction. If the member completes the trade, we consider it an accurate test trade. We don't actually do the trade because we do so many others. We clearly notify the member and apologize; we only consider it a success if the member agrees to do the trade. These are done on alternate accounts.
How do we find demand?
Our demand is extremely accurate and easy to obtain. To get it, we look at the number of messages sent with the vehicle in the previous three days on the official jailbreak discord server. The more conversations, trade posts, etc. there are, the higher the demand is.
Demand Calculations
To have precise rankings, our demand is calculated with a math formula that shows the demand out of 100. We use this to ensure everything is exactly correct.

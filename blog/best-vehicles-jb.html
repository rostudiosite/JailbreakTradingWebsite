Best Roblox Jailbreak Vehicles

Have you ever wondered what would be the best vehicle in jailbreak? Here you can find out what your next best vehicle would be! Here we are considering multiple different things, such as price, performance. This list will only include obtainable vehicles.

1. Roadster

Price: $600,000
Max Speed: 210 MPH

The roadster is great at everything! At a fair price, it offers great performance. It is the 6th fastest car currently for sale, with a top speed of 210 MPH! It is primarily known for its insane acceleration. Both its breaking and handling are excellent. The drawbacks include its slow reverse speed and poor off-road performance.

2. Eclaire

Price: $500,000
Max Speed: 280 MPH
Spawn Location: Near the bank at the badimo charging station.

The Eclaire is a great vehicle for those who want a high top speed for a reasonable price! It is the second fastest vehicle available in jailbreak, with a top speed of 280 MPH.

3. La Matador

Price: $100,000
Max Speed: 168 MPH
Spawn Location: Next to bank and at volcano criminal base.

This vehicle is the first super car you should get. It has a great price, good performance, great spawn locations, and nice looks. This was my first vehicle that I purchased and is one of my favorite vehicles.

4. Stallion

Price: $200,000
Max Speed: 240 MPH
Spawn Location: At the desert train station.

This vehicle is a great upgrade from the La Matador. It's basically the same but with better performance.

5. Model 3

Price: $16,000
Max Speed: 136 MPH
Spawn Location: At both badimo charging stations.

This vehicle is the best one to purchase for new jailbreak players. It is extremely good for its insanely low price and great performance. This is the must-buy vehicle for new players. It has been available from the start of jailbreak.
